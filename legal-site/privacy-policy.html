Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stream Team Spotlight (the "Extension") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when broadcasters and viewers use the Twitch Extension experience.

1. Scope

This policy applies to data processed through the Team Hub panel, spotlight overlay system, broadcaster configuration dashboard, and backend services supporting Extension features.

2. Information We Collect

  • Twitch user identifiers and display names
  • Profile metadata such as avatar image URLs
  • Stream status and category information
  • Stream team membership and shared team relationships
  • Broadcaster settings, custom bios, and trigger metadata
  • Technical diagnostics like error logs and timestamps

3. How We Use Information

  • Render panel and overlay spotlight experiences
  • Compute shared stream teams between creators
  • Deliver real-time spotlight events
  • Store broadcaster preferences and custom bios
  • Maintain service reliability and anti-abuse protections

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared only as needed to operate the service, including with Twitch APIs and infrastructure providers, or as legally required.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for operation, security, troubleshooting, and legal obligations.

6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards, but no internet service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your information.
