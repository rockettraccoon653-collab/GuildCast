# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stream Team Spotlight (the "Extension") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when broadcasters and viewers use the Twitch Extension experience.

By using the Extension, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to data processed through:

- The Team Hub panel
- The spotlight overlay system
- The broadcaster configuration dashboard
- The backend services that support Extension features

This policy does not cover Twitch's own data practices. Twitch account and platform data are also governed by Twitch's policies.

## 2. Information We Collect

Depending on feature use, the Extension may process:

- Twitch user identifiers and display names
- Profile metadata (such as avatar image URLs)
- Stream status and category/game information
- Stream team membership and shared team relationships
- Broadcaster settings (theme, trigger options, durations)
- Custom creator bios entered by a broadcaster
- Trigger event metadata (manual and supported event-driven spotlights)
- Technical diagnostics (error logs, timestamps, basic service metrics)

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Render panel and overlay spotlight experiences
- Compute shared stream teams between broadcaster and featured creators
- Deliver real-time spotlight events
- Store broadcaster preferences and custom bios
- Enforce reliability, anti-abuse controls, and rate limits
- Debug, monitor, and improve service performance

## 4. Legal Basis and Permissions

Where required by law, data is processed based on legitimate interests in operating and securing the service, contractual necessity for requested functionality, and/or user consent where applicable.

Broadcasters are responsible for ensuring they have rights and permissions for custom content they submit.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may share data only as needed to operate the service, including:

- Twitch platform APIs and services
- Hosting, database, caching, and observability providers
- Legal authorities when required by law

Service providers are expected to process data only for operational purposes.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for service operation, security, troubleshooting, and legal obligations.

- Configuration and custom bios are retained until changed or deleted by the broadcaster, or until account/channel offboarding
- Event and diagnostic logs are retained for a limited operational period
- Cached Twitch-derived data is periodically refreshed or expired

## 7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No internet service is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## 8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information.

Broadcasters can typically manage most Extension data by:

- Updating settings in the configuration dashboard
- Editing or removing custom bios
- Uninstalling or disabling the Extension

For legal/privacy requests, contact us using the details below.

## 9. International Processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we apply appropriate safeguards for cross-border processing.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe data was submitted inappropriately, contact us for review.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Effective Date" reflects the latest version. Continued use after updates means you acknowledge the revised policy.
